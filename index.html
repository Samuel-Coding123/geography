Pros and Cons of Future Population Growth

Pros:

  • Increased workforce to drive economic growth.
  • Diversification of culture and talent.
  • Potential for innovation and technological advancements.

Cons:

  • Increased strain on urban infrastructure and resources.
  • Higher housing demand leading to affordability issues.
  • Environmental challenges due to urban expansion.
